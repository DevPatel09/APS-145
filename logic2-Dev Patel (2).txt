step 1: Start

step 3: Two players challenged each other for a basketball shootout.
step 4: Both roll the dice simultaneously for determining who will shoot first.
step 5: If there is a tie (repeat step 4)
              If there is no tie (proceed to step 6)          
step 6: The one with highest number on the dice will go first for the shootout.
step 7: Then the second player will proceed for the shootouts.
step 8: End
